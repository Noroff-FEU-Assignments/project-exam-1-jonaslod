About The Library

'The Library' is a blog written by a book loving web developer. A place where I can post about all the different books I read, and how they've shaped the world we live in. The books I read vary in genre, everything from young-adult-fiction to political fiction. My goal for the blog is to share my thoughts, and invite discussion from users visiting.

Sounds interesting? Want to get an overview of all the books I've read? Explore my posts >>

I hope you find my thoughts interesting, and I invite you to explore my blog further.
